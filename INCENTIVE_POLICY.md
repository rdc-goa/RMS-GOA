## 1. Research Papers

The incentive for research papers is determined by a base amount, which is then adjusted based on publication type and author distribution.

### Base Incentive Amount

The base amount depends on the faculty member's designation and their faculty's category.

**PhD Scholars:**
Incentives are only provided for high-quartile publications.
- **Q1 Journal:** ₹6,000
- **Q2 Journal:** ₹4,000
- **Q3, Q4, or other:** ₹0

**Category 'A' Faculties:**
This special policy applies to faculty from the following disciplines:
- Faculty of Applied Sciences
- Faculty of Medicine
- Faculty of Homoeopathy
- Faculty of Ayurved
- Faculty of Nursing
- Faculty of Pharmacy
- Faculty of Physiotherapy
- Faculty of Public Health
- Faculty of Engineering & Technology

Incentives are based *only* on the journal's Scopus/WoS quartile rating:
- **Nature/Science/Lancet:** ₹50,000
- **Top 1% Journals:** ₹25,000
- **Q1 Journal:** ₹15,000
- **Q2 Journal:** ₹10,000
- **Q3 Journal:** ₹6,000
- **Q4 Journal:** ₹4,000

**All Other Faculties (Non-Category 'A'):**
Incentives are based on a broader range of indexing criteria:
- **Scopus/WoS (Q1-Q4):** Same as Category 'A' (e.g., Q1 is ₹15,000).
- **WoS (SCIE, SSCI, A&HCI):** ₹3,000
- **UGC-CARE Group-I:** ₹1,000
- **Scopus Indexed Conference Proceedings:** ₹3,000

### Adjustments to Base Amount

1.  **Publication Type Adjustment:** The base amount is adjusted based on the type of publication.
    - **Research Articles / Short Communications:** 100% of base amount.
    - **Scopus Indexed Conference Proceedings:** Flat ₹3,000 (not subject to further adjustments other than author share).
    - **Case Reports / Short Surveys:** 90% of base amount.
    - **Review Articles (Q3/Q4):** 80% of base amount. (Q1/Q2 review articles get 100%).
    - **Letter to the Editor/Editorial:** Flat total of ₹2,500 to be distributed among authors.

2.  **PU Goa Affiliation & APC Payment:**
    - If "Parul University Goa" is **not** mentioned in the publication affiliation, the final incentive is **reduced by 50%**.
    - If the Article Processing Charge (APC) was paid by the University, the final incentive is **reduced by 50%**.

### Author Distribution Rules

The adjusted total incentive is distributed among PU authors based on their roles.

- **Rule 1 (Sole Author):** If there is only one internal author (as First or Corresponding Author), they receive **100%** of the total incentive.
- **Rule 2 (Sole Co-Author):** If there is only one internal author (as Co-Author), they receive **80%** of the total incentive.
- **Rule 3 (Multiple Co-Authors Only):** If there are multiple internal Co-Authors (and no internal First/Corresponding Authors), **80%** of the total incentive is distributed equally among them.
- **Rule 4 (Mixed Roles):** If there are internal First/Corresponding Authors AND internal Co-Authors:
  - **70%** of the total incentive is distributed equally among the First/Corresponding Authors.
  - **30%** of the total incentive is distributed equally among the Co-Authors.
- **Conference Proceedings:** For Scopus Indexed Conference Proceedings, only the designated **Presenting Author(s)** are eligible. The incentive is distributed equally among all internal presenting authors.

## 2. Conference Presentations

The reimbursement for conference presentations depends on the event's location, mode (online/offline), and the applicant's presentation order in the calendar year (for online events).

**PU-Organized Conferences:**
- **Reimbursement:** 75% of the registration fee.
- **Frequency:** This can be claimed once per year.

**Other Conferences (Offline):**
The final reimbursement is the *lesser of* the total eligible expenses (Registration Fee + Travel Fare) and the maximum policy limit.
- **International (in India):**
  - Oral Presentation: Up to ₹20,000
  - Poster Presentation: Up to ₹15,000
- **National:**
  - Oral Presentation: Up to ₹12,000
  - Poster Presentation: Up to ₹10,000
- **Regional/State:**
  - Up to ₹7,500
- **International (outside India):**
  - Indian Subcontinent: Up to ₹30,000
  - South Korea, Japan, Australia, Middle East: Up to ₹45,000
  - Europe: Up to ₹60,000
  - Africa, South/North America, Other: Up to ₹75,000

**Other Conferences (Online):**
The final reimbursement is the *lesser of* the calculated registration fee percentage and the maximum policy limit.
- **First Presentation of the year:** 75% of registration fee, up to ₹15,000
- **Second Presentation:** 60% of registration fee, up to ₹10,000
- **Third Presentation:** 50% of registration fee, up to ₹7,000
- **Additional Presentations:** 30% of registration fee, up to ₹2,000

## 3. Books & Book Chapters

The base incentive depends on the publication type (Book/Chapter), publisher type, and page count.

**Book Chapters:**
- **Scopus Indexed:** ₹6,000
- **International Publisher:**
  - >20 pages: ₹3,000
  - 10-20 pages: ₹2,000
  - 5-9 pages: ₹1,000
- **National Publisher:**
  - >20 pages: ₹2,500
  - 10-20 pages: ₹1,500
  - 5-9 pages: ₹500

**Full Books:**
- **Scopus Indexed:** ₹18,000
- **International Publisher:**
  - >350 pages: ₹6,000
  - 200-350 pages: ₹3,500
  - <200 pages: ₹2,000
- **National Publisher:**
  - >350 pages: ₹3,000
  - 200-350 pages: ₹2,500
  - 100-199 pages: ₹2,000
  - <100 pages: ₹1,000

### Adjustments & Distribution

- **Editor Role:** If the applicant's role is "Editor," the base incentive is **reduced by 50%**.
- **Multiple Authors:** The final calculated amount is distributed equally among all internal (PU) authors.
- **Multiple Chapters (Same Book):** If an author publishes 'n' chapters in the same book, the total incentive is the sum of (base_incentive / k) for k=1 to n, capped at the incentive for publishing a full book.

## 4. Patents

The incentive for patents is based on the patent's current status and whether PU is an applicant. The amount is distributed equally among all inventors.

**Base Amount (per patent):**
- **Published:** ₹3,000
- **Granted:** ₹15,000
- **Filed:** ₹0

### Adjustments

- **PU Goa as Sole Applicant:** 100% of the base amount is used for distribution.
- **PU Goa as Joint Applicant:** 80% of the base amount is used for distribution.
- **PU Goa Not an Applicant:** 0% incentive.

---

## 5. Seed Money for APC

Reimbursement for Article Processing Charges (APC) is calculated as the *lesser of* the actual APC paid and the maximum policy limit, distributed equally among all PU Goa authors.

**Maximum Reimbursement Limit:**
- **Q1 Journal:** ₹40,000
- **Q2 Journal:** ₹30,000
- **Q3 Journal:** ₹20,000
- **Q4 Journal:** ₹15,000
- **WoS (ESCI):** ₹8,000 (Not applicable for Category 'A' faculties)
- **UGC-CARE Group-I:** ₹5,000 (Not applicable for Category 'A' faculties)

**Important:** To be eligible, the author must provide proof that they first requested a waiver for the APC from the publisher.

---

## 6. Membership of Professional Bodies

- **Incentive:** 50% of the membership fee paid.
- **Cap:** The maximum incentive is capped at ₹10,000.
- **Frequency:** A faculty member can claim this incentive once per year.
